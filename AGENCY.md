## What We Do — Services

### 🖥️ Modern Web Development *(Core)*
Marketing sites, dashboards, portals, and custom apps in modern stacks. Fast, clear, and maintainable. We pick the right rendering strategy (static, server, or hybrid) based on your content and traffic — not trends.

**Tech we use:** Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, GSAP

---

### 🤖 AI Automation Systems *(Core)*
Chatbots, workflow automation, AI assistants, CRM/support pipelines, and custom integrations — built with retries, observability, and safety guardrails. Not fragile scripts.

**Tech we use:** OpenAI APIs, FastAPI, Supabase, Make.com, LangChain, TypeScript / Python

---

### 🎨 UI/UX Design *(Core)*
Product UI, dashboards, responsive systems, and landing pages — scoped to engineering constraints so designs actually ship. We extend your tokens and work within your brand identity, not against it.

**Tech we use:** Figma, Design tokens, Component specs, Motion UX, WCAG-minded patterns

---

### 🛒 E-Commerce Solutions *(Secondary)*
Shopify themes, custom Liquid storefronts, headless commerce — checkout-first, performance-first, no heavy builders. We also handle platform migrations with full SEO continuity.

**Tech we use:** Shopify, Liquid, Headless commerce, React, Tailwind CSS

### ⌨️ WordPress Development *(Secondary)*
Lean WordPress and WooCommerce with custom themes, native Gutenberg blocks, and security-first hardening. No Elementor. No Divi. Just clean, portable, fast code.

## Our Process

Every engagement follows the same three-phase rhythm:

1. **Discovery & Scoping** — Goals, audiences, routes, integrations, and failure modes mapped before any design or build work begins.
2. **Build & Integrate** — Typed, testable components; real APIs; staging environments. We write code that survives real traffic.
3. **Launch & Measure** — Performance budgets, monitoring, analytics wiring, and iteration on real usage data.
